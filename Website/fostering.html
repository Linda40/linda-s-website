    Why would you want to foster a dog?

    By fostering any animal, you are helping to save their lives, by removing them from shelters. As a foster parent you are helping us to understand the animal needs which in return will help with their adoption. As a foster parent you will be training them to enter a new home, by observing their behavior and training.

    Responsibilities while fostering:

    All pets must be feed two to three times a day based on their age and weight, fresh water must be available for pets at all times. A warm and dry place to sleep if not create trained. Medications given flea/tick, heartworm preventive and any other medications they my be on at the time.
